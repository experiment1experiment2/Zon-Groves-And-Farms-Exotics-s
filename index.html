Discover a World of Wings

Welcome to Zon Groves and Farms' aviary, where you can encounter a stunning collection of exotic birds from around the globe. We provide a responsible and loving environment for these feathered friends, and we're excited to share their beauty and wonder with you.

Featured Birds:

Macaw

With their vibrant plumage and playful personalities, macaws are some of the most captivating birds in the world. We have several species at Zon Groves and Farms, each with its own unique charm.

Toucan

Easily recognizable by their oversized beaks, toucans are fascinating creatures from the rainforests of South America. Their playful antics and colorful coats make them a joy to observe.

Elegant and graceful, flamingos are a sight to behold. We have a small flock of these long-legged beauties at Zon Groves and Farms, adding a touch of pink to our aviary.
